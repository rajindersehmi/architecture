About us

To design something that one would want to be a part of, something one would want to visit and enjoy in an attempt to improve one's quality of life.” – Ar. Frank Gehry.

Design Labs is a budding architecture practice that intends to be young, innovative, and ambitious. Conceived by Gurnoor Bolaria and Ravinder Singh, as the principals, the studio looks forward to growing into a highly collaborative practice, with a core group of design associates who bring varied skill sets to the process. We thrive to grasp the essence of intangible aspects of one’s need into our tangible designs resulting in engaging architecture. We look at all the projects to their ultimate capacity through our design With experience in architecture, urban planning, landscape design, and interior design the studio intends to create a niche in the preoccupied market through our designs.

our goal

Pushing Boundaries, Inspiring Dreams At Design Labs, we're not just architects — we're dreamers, innovators, and creators. With six years of experience shaping spaces and landscapes, our goal remains steadfast: to push the boundaries of architectural design, inspiring awe and igniting imaginations with every project we undertake. Leading the Way in Sustainability We believe in architecture that not only captivates the eye but also nurtures the planet.
